Tags

You can use them in captions and watermark.
[MISC:PhotoNumber]The number of the photo displayed.
[FILE:Name]Full name of the photo file.
[FILE:BaseName]Name of the photo file, without its extention.
[EXIF:CameraMaker]Maker of the camera used.
[EXIF:CameraModel]Model of the camera used.
[EXIF:Year]Shooting year of the photo.
[EXIF:Date]Shooting date of the photo: day/month/year.
[EXIF:Time]Shooting time of the photo: hours:mintues:seconds.
[EXIF:FocalLength]Focal length used.
[EXIF:FNumber]Aperture used.
[EXIF:ExposureTime]Time of exposure.
[EXIF:ExposureProgram]Exposure program used.
[EXIF:Iso]ISO.
[EXIF:Flash]Flash mode.
[IPTC:Caption]A description of the photo.
[IPTC:Country]Country where the photo was shot.
[IPTC:City]City where the photo was shot.
[IPTC:Sublocation]More precise location where the photo was shot.
[IPTC:Keywords]Keywords.
[IPTC:Author]Author of the photo.
[IPTC:Credit]Embedded credits.
[IPTC:Source]Source of the photo.
[IPTC:Copyright]Copyright notice.
[IPTC:Contact]How to contact the author.

Note that depending on the software used to process the photos, some EXIF and IPTC tags may not be present in your files.
